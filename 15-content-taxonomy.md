# Content Taxonomy

Version: 1.0

Project: Sumanglam Digital Showroom Platform

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the official content hierarchy of the Sumanglam platform.

It acts as the source of truth for:

* Navigation
* CMS Structure
* Category Pages
* Product Organization
* Search
* Filtering
* SEO Architecture

---

# 2. Taxonomy Philosophy

The platform is organized around how customers think.

Customers think:

Space
↓
Category
↓
Brand
↓
Product

Not:

Brand
↓
Product

Therefore the taxonomy prioritizes:

* Spaces
* Solutions
* Categories
* Inspirations

before products.

# 12. Governance Rules

New categories should only be created when:

* Multiple products exist
* Customer demand exists
* Long-term relevance is expected

Avoid category proliferation.

Maintain a clean and understandable taxonomy.
