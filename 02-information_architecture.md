# Information Architecture (IA)

Version: 1.0
Project: Sumanglam Digital Showroom Platform
Status: Draft

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the information architecture, navigation structure, page hierarchy, user journeys, and content organization for the Sumanglam website.

The website is not intended to function primarily as an ecommerce platform.

Its primary purpose is to:

- Build trust
- Inspire homeowners
- Showcase expertise
- Present premium brands
- Educate customers
- Drive showroom visits
- Generate consultation bookings
- Facilitate WhatsApp inquiries

---

# 2. Brand Positioning

## Primary Brand

Sumanglam

Sumanglam is positioned as a premium destination for:

- Modular Kitchens
- Wardrobes
- Premium Hardware
- Luxury Appliances
- Interior Solutions

The website should communicate:

- Trust
- Expertise
- Modernity
- Customization
- Premium Quality

The website should not position Sumanglam as a generic hardware retailer.

---

## Brand Ecosystem

### Sumanglam

Showroom, expertise, consultation, experience.

### Nolte

Type:

Solution Brand

Focus:

* Modular Kitchens
* Customized Interior Solutions
* Luxury Homes
* Personalized Design

Description:

Nolte is a well renowned German Luxury Kitchen Solutions brand focusing on desigining and delivering luxurious modular kitchens.

While hardware and accessories may be sourced from premium partners such as Hettich, Blum, and Hafele, the overall design, planning, manufacturing, and solution delivery are owned by the Mrida ecosystem.

Hardware, Appliances and accessories are sourced from premium brands already partnered with Sumanglam, and the overall design, planning, manufacturing and solution delivery are done by Nolte.

Nolte stands as a trademark in luxury interior solutions in the modular kitchen space.

## Mrida

Type:

Solution Brand

Focus:

* Modular Kitchens
* Modular Wardrobes
* Customized Interior Solutions
* Indian Homes
* Personalized Design

Description:

Mrida is Sumanglam's in-house solution brand focused on designing and delivering customized modular kitchens and wardrobes.

While hardware and accessories may be sourced from premium partners such as Hettich, Blum, and Hafele, the overall design, planning, manufacturing, and solution delivery are owned by the Mrida ecosystem.

Mrida represents Sumanglam's long-term vision of creating complete interior solutions tailored to individual homes and lifestyles.


### Hafele

Premium Hardware, kitchen appliances and accessories

### Bosch

Premium Kitchen appliances.

### Siemens

Premium Kitchen appliances.

### Liebherr

Premium appliances, specifically refrigeration.

# 11. Mobile-First Principles

The website should be designed mobile-first.

Priorities:

- Fast loading
- Large imagery
- Simple navigation
- Accessible CTAs
- Smooth scrolling experience

Desktop experience should extend the mobile experience, not define it.
