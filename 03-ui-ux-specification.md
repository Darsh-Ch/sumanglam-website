# Homepage Specification

---

# 1. Purpose

The homepage serves as the primary entry point into the Sumanglam ecosystem.

Its purpose is not to sell products directly.

Its purpose is to:

- Build trust
- Create aspiration
- Showcase expertise
- Introduce Sumanglam
- Encourage exploration
- Drive showroom visits
- Generate consultation requests

---

# 2. Primary Audience

## Primary

Homeowners

Users planning:

- New homes
- Renovations
- Modular kitchens
- Wardrobes
- Interior upgrades

---

## Secondary

Architects

Interior Designers

Builders

---

## Tertiary

Product researchers looking for:

- Hardware
- Appliances
- Premium brands

---

# 3. Emotional Goals

Visitors should feel:

1. Luxury
2. Trust
3. Warmth
4. Modernity
5. Customization
6. Expertise

The website should communicate:

"We can help bring your dream home to life."

The website should not communicate:

"We are a hardware shop."

Highest Priority

---

# 14. Desktop Experience

Use whitespace aggressively.

Large imagery.

Editorial layouts.

Sophisticated scrolling experience.

Never overcrowd sections.

---

# 15. Image Strategy

Homepage imagery should prioritize:

1. Professional kitchen visuals
2. Official brand assets
3. High-quality renders
4. Professionally edited showroom photography

Avoid:

Low-quality phone photography in hero sections.

---

# 16. Animation Rules

Default:

Subtle

---

Allowed

Fade

Slide

Reveal

Gentle Parallax

---

Avoid

Heavy motion

Auto-playing disruptive effects

Excessive animation chains
