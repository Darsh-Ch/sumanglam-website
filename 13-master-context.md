# Master Context

Project: Sumanglam Digital Showroom Platform

Version: 1.0

# Overview

Sumanglam is a premium showroom and interior solutions destination.

The business focuses on:

* Modular Kitchens
* Wardrobes
* Premium Hardware
* Luxury Appliances

The website is not an ecommerce platform.

Its primary purpose is to:

* Inspire homeowners
* Build trust
* Showcase expertise
* Generate showroom visits
* Generate consultation requests

---

## Navigation Philosophy

Users should generally discover products through:

Category
↓
Brand
↓
Product

rather than:

Brand
↓
Product

This aligns with how customers typically search for solutions in the hardware and kitchen industry.
