# CTA System

## CTA Priority

1. Explore Inspirations.
2. Visit Showroom.
3. Book Consultation.
4. WhatsApp Inquiry.
5. Contact Us.

## CTA Roles

Explore Inspirations:

* Primary discovery CTA.
* Best for homepage hero and exploration sections.

Visit Showroom:

* Primary physical conversion CTA.
* Best for showroom and high-trust sections.

Book Consultation:

* Main lead-capture CTA.
* Best after inspiration, brand, product, kitchen, wardrobe, and showroom context.

WhatsApp Inquiry:

* High-intent conversational CTA.
* Should generate context-aware messages.

Contact Us:

* Lowest priority general communication CTA.

## Implementation Rules

* CTAs should not feel aggressive.
* Critical CTAs must remain visible and usable on mobile.
* WhatsApp should be persistent or easily accessible on mobile.
* CTAs should preserve source tracking where they lead to consultation or WhatsApp.
