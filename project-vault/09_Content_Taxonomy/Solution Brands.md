# Solution Brands

## Purpose

Defines brands that provide complete design systems rather than individual products.

## Nolte

Type: Solution Brand.

Focus:

* Premium German Kitchens.
* Design Systems.
* Luxury Living Spaces.

Rules:

* Nolte is kitchen-focused.
* Do not imply Nolte wardrobes.

## Mrida

Type: Solution Brand.

Focus:

* Modular Kitchens.
* Premium Wardrobes.
* Customized Solutions.
* Indian Homes.
* Personalized Design.

Rules:

* Wardrobes belong to Mrida.
* Do not model wardrobes as independent product catalogs.
