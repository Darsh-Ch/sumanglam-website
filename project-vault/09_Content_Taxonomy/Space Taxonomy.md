# Space Taxonomy

## Purpose

Defines the official high-level spaces used for navigation, CMS structure, category pages, search, filtering, and SEO.

## Spaces

### Kitchen

Includes:

* Modular Kitchens.
* Kitchen Inspirations.
* Appliances.
* Kitchen Hardware.
* Storage Solutions.

Associated brands:

* Nolte.
* Mrida.
* Bosch.
* Siemens.
* Liebherr.
* Hafele.
* Blaupunkt.
* Hettich.
* Blum.
* Spitze.
* Everyday.

### Wardrobe

Represents wardrobe and storage systems.

Associated brand:

* Mrida.

### Hardware

Represents hardware and fittings.

Associated brands:

* Hafele.
* Hettich.
* Blum.
* Godrej.
* Dorset.
* Yale.
* Brass Barony.
* Spitze.
* Everyday.

### Appliances

Represents premium kitchen appliances.

Associated brands:

* Bosch.
* Siemens.
* Liebherr.
* Hafele.
* Blaupunkt.

## Taxonomy Philosophy

Customers think Space -> Category -> Brand -> Product. Do not make brand-first product browsing the main structure.
