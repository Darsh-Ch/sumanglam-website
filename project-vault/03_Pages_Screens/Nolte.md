# Nolte

## Purpose

Showcase Nolte as a premium German kitchen solution brand and build trust through design philosophy, collections, inspirations, and kitchen solution storytelling.

## User Goal

Evaluate Nolte as a premium kitchen option and decide whether to view inspirations, visit the showroom, or book a kitchen consultation.

## Entry Points

* [[Kitchens]]
* [[Brands]]
* Homepage Featured Brands.
* Related inspiration pages.

## Exit Points

* Nolte kitchen inspirations.
* [[Product Detail]] for related products if appropriate.
* [[Book Consultation]]
* [[Showroom Experience]]
* [[WhatsApp Inquiry]]

## UI Requirements

* Brand hero.
* Design philosophy.
* Kitchen collections.
* Inspirations.
* Customization possibilities.
* Brand storytelling.
* CTAs for showroom visit and consultation.

## UX Behavior

* Keep Nolte premium and kitchen-focused.
* Use large imagery and short copy.
* Avoid treating Nolte as just a filter tag.

## Content Requirements

* Nolte brand story.
* Premium German kitchen positioning.
* Kitchen inspiration and collection links.
* Showroom floor relation if available.

## Data Requirements

* [[Database - brands]] with `brand_type = solution`.
* [[Database - inspirations]] linked through [[Database - junction tables]].
* [[Database - showroom_sections]] for Nolte floor.

## API/backend Requirements

* [[API - Brands]].
* [[API - Inspirations]].
* [[API - Showroom]].

## Auth/permission Rules

Public page.

## Edge Cases

* Missing official Nolte assets.
* No linked inspirations.
* User expects wardrobe content.

## Forbidden Mistakes

* Do not imply Nolte wardrobes.
* Do not treat Nolte and Mrida as identical brands.
* Do not make the page a logo wall or product filter only.
