Present Sumanglam's story, philosophy, values, and expertise.

Understand who Sumanglam is and why they are trustworthy.

* Footer.
* Trust-building links from homepage.

## UI Requirements

* Company background.
* Philosophy.
* Values.
* Expertise.

## UX Behavior

* Warm, premium, human, concise.
* Avoid inflated claims or generic marketing.

## Content Requirements

* Sumanglam story.
* Premium showroom positioning.
* Trust and expertise copy.

## Data Requirements

Static content or CMS content, depending on implementation decision.

## API/backend Requirements

No specific API documented unless content is CMS-managed.

## Auth/permission Rules

Public page.

## Edge Cases

* Missing final company story.
* Unverified claims.

## Forbidden Mistakes

* Do not use exaggerated claims.
* Do not use fake metrics.
* Do not make it feel like generic corporate copy.
