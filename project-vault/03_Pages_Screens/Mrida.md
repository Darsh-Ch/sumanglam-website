# Mrida

## Purpose

Showcase Mrida as Sumanglam's in-house solution brand for modular kitchens, modular wardrobes, customized interior solutions, Indian homes, and personalized design.

## User Goal

Understand Mrida's kitchen and wardrobe solution capabilities and move toward inspiration, showroom visit, or consultation.

## Entry Points

* [[Kitchens]]
* [[Wardrobes]]
* [[Brands]]
* Homepage Featured Brands.
* Related inspiration pages.

## Exit Points

* Mrida kitchen inspirations.
* Mrida wardrobe inspirations.
* [[Book Consultation]]
* [[Showroom Experience]]
* [[WhatsApp Inquiry]]

## UI Requirements

* Brand hero.
* Story and design philosophy.
* Modular kitchen section.
* Modular wardrobe section.
* Customization journey.
* Inspirations and collections.
* CTAs for consultation and showroom.

## UX Behavior

* Position Mrida as a complete interior solutions brand.
* Keep wardrobe content under Mrida.
* Make customization and local expertise clear.

## Content Requirements

* Mrida story.
* Modular kitchen content.
* Modular wardrobe content.
* Customized interior solution copy.
* Related inspirations and showroom floor.

Public page.

## Edge Cases

* Missing wardrobe imagery.
* Unclear distinction from Nolte.
* Generic product catalog treatment.

## Forbidden Mistakes

* Do not model wardrobes as independent product catalogs.
* Do not make Mrida identical to Nolte.
* Do not reduce Mrida to a product brand.
