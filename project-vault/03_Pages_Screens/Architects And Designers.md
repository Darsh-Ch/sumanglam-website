# Architects And Designers

## Purpose

Support professional collaboration without building the excluded Architect Portal.

## User Goal

Evaluate Sumanglam's brand ecosystem, technical support, and collaboration potential, then schedule a discussion.

## Entry Points

* Main navigation.
* Footer.
* Brand/product research paths.

## Exit Points

* Schedule Discussion.
* [[Book Consultation]]
* [[Contact]]
* [[WhatsApp Inquiry]]
* Brand pages.

## UI Requirements

* Partnership opportunity content.
* Consultation support.
* Brand ecosystem overview.
* Technical resource request CTA if available.
* Schedule Discussion CTA.

## UX Behavior

* Professional and practical.
* Avoid consumer-only language.
* Do not imply a full portal, dashboard, or account system in V1.

## Content Requirements

* Professional collaboration messaging.
* Brand ecosystem links.
* Technical support/consultation copy.

* [[Database - brands]]
* [[Database - leads]]
* [[Database - consultations]]

## API/backend Requirements

* [[API - Brands]]
* [[API - Consultations]]
* [[API - Analytics Events]]

## Auth/permission Rules

Public page. No professional account in V1.

## Edge Cases

* User expects downloadable technical catalog.
* User expects login or portal.

## Forbidden Mistakes

* Do not build Architect Portal or trade dashboard in V1.
* Do not introduce user accounts.
