# Product Summary

## Purpose

Sumanglam is a premium digital showroom and interior discovery platform for modular kitchens, wardrobes, premium hardware, luxury appliances, and interior solutions.

The website should bridge emotional inspiration and technical research. It should help visitors visualize spaces, understand premium brands and products, and take the next step toward a showroom visit, WhatsApp inquiry, or consultation.

## Positioning

Sumanglam should be positioned as:

* A premium destination for kitchens, wardrobes, hardware, appliances, and interior solutions.
* A trusted local expert.
* A design and consultation partner.
* A showroom experience worth visiting.

Sumanglam should not be positioned as:

* A generic hardware retailer.
* An ecommerce marketplace.
* A discount retailer.
* A technology startup.
* A design agency.

## Core Discovery Philosophy

The governing discovery model is inspiration-first:

Space -> Collection -> Inspiration -> Brand/Product -> Consultation.

Products support spaces. Spaces do not exist merely to sell products.

## Success Metrics

* Consultation requests.
* WhatsApp inquiries.
* Showroom visits.
* Inspiration engagement.
* Brand page engagement.
* Product page engagement.
* Qualified B2B inbound leads.
