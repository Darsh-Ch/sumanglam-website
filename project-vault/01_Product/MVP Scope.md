* User accounts.
* Wishlists.
* Saved products.
* Saved inspirations.
* Quotation generator.
* Advanced filtering.
* Supplier PDF ingestion.
* Full product catalog.
* Trade dashboard.
* Architect Portal.
* Recommendation engine.

## Phase Notes

Phase 1 emphasizes premium presence: brand trust, inspiration gallery, Nolte and Mrida solution pages, and showroom conversion.

Phase 2 introduces a curated product catalog.

Phase 3 introduces bulk supplier PDF ingestion.

Phase 4 expands B2B, wishlist, quotation, and advanced designer flows.
