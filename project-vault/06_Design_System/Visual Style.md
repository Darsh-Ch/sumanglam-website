# Visual Style

## Brand Personality

Sumanglam should feel:

* Luxury.
* Trustworthy.
* Warm.
* Modern.
* Sophisticated.
* Knowledgeable.
* Approachable.

## Visual Mix

* Luxury Showroom: 50 percent.
* Apple Minimalism: 35 percent.
* Editorial Design: 15 percent.

## Layout Mood

* Curated, not stacked.
* Spacious, not empty.
* Calm, not sleepy.
* Premium, not decorative.

## Imagery Priority

1. Professional interiors.
2. Kitchen environments.
3. Wardrobes.
4. Product close-ups.
5. Showroom imagery.

## Photography Style

Preferred:

* Natural lighting.
* Warm lighting.
* Architectural framing.
* Premium materials.
* Human-scaled spaces.

Avoid:

* Flash photography.
* Over-saturated colors.
* Busy compositions.
* Low-quality mobile photography.
* Stock-photo aesthetics.

## Forbidden Visuals

* Glassmorphism.
* Neon colors.
* Cyberpunk aesthetics.
* Excessive gradients.
* Dark tech startup themes.
* Template-style layouts.
* Aggressive popups.
* Overcrowded sections.
