# Typography

## Philosophy

Typography should feel elegant, modern, confident, and easy to read.

## Headline Rules

* Short.
* Impactful.
* Minimal.
* Clear hierarchy.

## Body Copy Rules

* Simple.
* Helpful.
* Human.
* Clear.
* Every sentence should earn its place.

## Copy Direction

Prefer concise, meaningful copy such as "Designed Around Your Home" over long marketing paragraphs.

## Open Questions

* Exact font family is not specified.
* Font scale and token names are not specified.
