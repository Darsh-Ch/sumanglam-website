# Do Not Do

Project: Sumanglam Digital Showroom Platform

---

# Brands

Do not model wardrobes as independent product catalogs.

Do not treat Nolte and Mrida as identical solution brands.

---

# Architecture

Do not introduce:

* Microservices
* Separate backend services
* Redis
* Kafka
* Elasticsearch
* Unapproved dependencies

---

# UI

Do not use:

* Glassmorphism
* Neon colors
* Dark cyberpunk themes
* Excessive gradients
* Overcrowded layouts

---

# UX

Do not prioritize products over inspirations.

Do not design the website like an ecommerce store.

Do not create cluttered navigation.

---

# Components

Do not create duplicate components.

Do not hardcode content.

Do not create page-specific components if a reusable version is possible.

---

# State Management

Do not introduce Redux.

Do not introduce complex state management unless absolutely necessary.

---

# Database

Do not create tables outside documented domain models.

Do not denormalize data without justification.

Do not store duplicate information.

---

# APIs

Do not create undocumented endpoints.

Do not return inconsistent response formats.

---

# Forms

Do not create forms without validation.

Do not trust client-side validation alone.

---

# Images

Do not serve original full-resolution images.

Do not use unoptimized image tags.

---

# Mobile

Do not design desktop-first.

Do not hide critical actions on mobile.

---

# Content

Do not use generic marketing copy.

Do not use exaggerated claims.

Do not use fake urgency tactics.

---

# Future Features

Do not build:

* User Accounts
* Wishlists
* Quotation Engine
* Architect Portal

unless explicitly scheduled for a later phase.
